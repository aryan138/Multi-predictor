About Titanic Dataset


Source of the Dataset


The Titanic Dataset is sourced from Kaggle.


Definition of Variables


  • PassengerId: Unique identifier for each passenger.
  • Survived: Did the passenger survive (1) or perish (0)?
  • Pclass: Passenger class (1st, 2nd, or 3rd)
  • Sex: Passenger's gender (male or female)
  • Age: Passenger's age in years
  • SibSp: Number of siblings/spouses aboard (excluding spouse)
  • Parch: Number of parents/children aboard (excluding spouse)
  • Ticket: Unique ticket number
  • Fare: Ticket price
  • Cabin: Cabin number
  • Embarked: Port of embarkation (C, Q, or S)
