About Ames Housing Dataset


Source of the Dataset


The Ames Housing Dataset is sourced from Kaggle.


Definition of Variables


    Ames Housing dataset is a big dataset consisting of around 81 columns out of which used in this project are as follows:


  • Overall Qual: Overall material and finish quality of the house.
  • Gr Liv Area: Above grade (ground) living area square feet.
  • Garage Area: Size of garage in square feet.
  • Total Bsmt SF: Total square feet of basement area.
  • 1st Flr SF: First floor square feet.
  • Year Built: Original construction date.
  • Year Remod/Add: Remodel date (same as construction date if no remodeling or additions).
  • Full Bath: Full bathrooms above grade.
  • TotRms AbvGrd: Total rooms above grade (does not include bathrooms).
  • Neighborhood: Physical locations within Ames city limits.
  • SalePrice: Selling price of house
